Classic Fudgy Brownies

About This Recipe

These brownies are my personal favorite. They have a soft, chewy center with a slightly crisp top. If you love chocolate, this recipe is a must-try!

This version is inspired by a recipe from Preppy Kitchen .

~Ingredients~

  • 1 cup (225g) butter
  • 2 cups (400g) sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (120g) flour
  • 1 cup (85g) cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt

~Instructions~

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Melt the butter, then mix it with the sugar until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, cocoa powder, and salt.
  5. Slowly mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients.
  6. Pour the batter into a prepared pan and spread evenly.
    Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  7. Let cool before cutting.

~Tips~

For extra flavor, try adding chocolate chips or nuts. Be careful not to overbake. I personally underbake mine a little, it keeps them fudgy.
