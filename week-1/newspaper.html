A Fake Michael Jackson's Big Break: A Fake Trial

It takes a mere 45 minuites for Edward Moss to morph
from a little-known, Los Angeles-based actor
into one of the world's most infamous performers.

"I grow a lot of facial hair," Mr.Moss said with a sheepish, high-pitched giggle.
He then moves on to his olive complexion, which, with the help of MAC and La Femme makeup, is quickly made several shades lighter. Skin sufficently whitened, he heads for the eyes.

"I make them lower and a little more almond-shaped," he explained.
