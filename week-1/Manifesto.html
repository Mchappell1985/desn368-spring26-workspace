.:Mac's Coding Manifesto:.

Beginning Statement

This manifesto is an outline of my personal approach to this course, especially as someone who is already well-versed in coding. Even though I have the understanding that there is an emerging relevance in AI tools, I prioritize direct engagement for my learning process over automated assistance.

Pre-existing Knowledge

(1.) I am entering this course with a relatively strong coding background. With certification in C++ and Javascript. This may shape how I go about structuring my assignments, but I will follow criteria for grading. (2.) It seems basic execution for the coding portion will be easy for me, I will use this class to hone my pre-existing skills.

Personal Values

(3.) Any code writing for assignments will be completed by my own hand, unless project criteria directly requires AI assistance, I believe this is crucial to devloping true understanding. (4.) The necessary process of debugging and refinement towards my projects are important to me.

Feelings Towards AI

(5.) I still approach AI with consistent skepticism, especially in creative learning environments. (6.) While this is still my own opinion, the growing overreliance on generative AI is a risk in weakening overall comprehension.

Participation for Course Requirements

(7.) Despite the lingering feeelings I have towards AI, I will engage with it in an attempt to understand what purpose it could have as a creative assistance tool. (8.) I intend to use AI critically, rather than passively.

Broadening My Design Experience

(9.) I will admit, I am excited for some of the upcoming projects in this class, I intend to put forth my best effort when completing them. (10.) My goal is to create finished products that reflect my own understanding and effort that I retain from this course.
