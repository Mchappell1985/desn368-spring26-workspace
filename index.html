About Me

Designing with Purpose

✦ Who I Am ✦

I am a Visual Communication Design student at Eastern Washington University pursuing a Bachelor's degree with a minor in Animation and Motion Design. My creative background combines graphic design, illustration, web development, and visual storytelling, I approach projects from both a visual and strategic perspective. Through my coursework and personal projects, I have developed skills in design systems, digital illustration, front-end web design, and creative problem solving. I enjoy creating work that is both visually engaging and purposeful, with a focus on clear communication and memorable experiences for your brand.

✦ What I Do ✦

I specialize in visual communication design, illustration, branding, and front-end web design. Much of my creative work revolves around character design and storytelling, where I develop original characters, worlds, and visual narratives that tackle complex themes. These projects have strengthened my skills in design thinking, audience engagement, and creative problem solving. Whether I am building a website, developing a brand identity, or illustrating a character, I aim to create work that feels expressive, thoughtful, and authentic while maintaining strong design fundamentals.
